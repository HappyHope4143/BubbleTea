## Information Collection and Use

BubbleTea ("the App") is an Android mobile application developed by [Developer/Company Name]. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect the following information:

1. **Device Information**
   - Device model and manufacturer
   - Android operating system version
   - Unique device identifiers
   - App version information

2. **Usage Information**
   - App usage patterns
   - Screen view counts
   - Feature usage statistics
   - Error and crash reports

3. **Internet Activity**
   - News API requests (via NewsAPI.org)
   - Network connection status

### Advertising-Related Information

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Advertising ID (AAID)
- IP address
- Ad click and view data
- Device information and location (approximate)

AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Information We Do NOT Collect

The App does **NOT** collect:

- Personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number)
- Precise location information (GPS)
- Personal data (contacts, photos, files)
- User account information

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used for:

1. **Providing App Features**
   - Delivering technology news content (via NewsAPI.org)
   - Providing news widget functionality
   - Offline news caching (Room database)

2. **App Improvement**
   - Bug fixes and error correction
   - Performance optimization
   - User experience enhancement

3. **Advertising**
   - Displaying personalized ads (Google AdMob)
   - Measuring ad performance

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: News data is stored locally on your device (Room database)
- **Cache Management**: Only the latest 100 news items are automatically maintained; older data is automatically deleted
- **Data Encryption**: Data is encrypted during network transmission via HTTPS
